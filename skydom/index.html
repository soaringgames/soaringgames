Play as a young hero in an epic campaign in this fantasy world. Face your fears and march into battle, or learn to tame and ride a dragon. Battle hordes of underlings and evil dragons. Protect the world with swords, bows, dragons, and grim determination. Explore and adventure. Find your own path in a rich and diverse world. Discover new challenges in your quest to defeat the overlord and his underlings.

please know that skydom is a small indie experience built by, well, just me, with a bit of help from my friends to build out the music and storyline. i'm just a college kid with big aspirations, but if you're looking for a hundred-hour aaa campaign, this game isn't for you. if, however, you're willing to enjoy a more personal experience that might not be as vast as others, i would love your support :) also know that your money isn't going to a big company who wants to line their pockets, it's going to a real person who just wants to survive college and make it in life ;)
