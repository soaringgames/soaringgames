Unboxing Snarfles

Your box will come full of custom designed, 3d printed models. Let's take a look inside, shall we?

This is Snarfles. He's at the top of the food chain, and he eats...well, everything. A turn counter at his feet keeps track of where you are in your journey, but watch out for coming storms!

Each biome is fully modular, allowing you to create new boards which unlock their own possibilities. Experiment with different layouts, or try picking completely randomly! You never know what the world of Snarfles may throw at you... With Snarfles himself, 6 predators, 8 bunnies, 12 grass, and 11 biomes, the possibilities are endless.
