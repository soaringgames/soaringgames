What is Snarfles?

Snarfles is a single-player casual turn-based strategy game primarily for PC where you: Orchestrate predators, bunnies, and grass to keep an ecosystem alive Offer sacrifices to Snarfles, a mysterious overlord Strategize in a way that feels casual and accessible to anyone Currently in the development stage, we aim to release on PC with campaign and challenge modes after a 6 month development period.

Core Loop

Move

Position animals strategically to keep the ecosystem balanced

Eat/Reproduce

Build the food chain by eating other animals and reproducing

Sacrifice

Appease the Snarfles overlord by offering mandatory sacrifices

Survive

Keep your ecosystem alive for a set number of turns

Market Positioning & Comparables

Snarfles sits at the intersection of cozy games, accessible strategy, and emerging auto-battler mechanics. The game is designed for both genre hobbyists and casual players curious about games like Into the Breach, Inscryption, or Baba Is You but who find most strategy games intimidating or stressful.

Comparables:

  • Baba Is You (indie puzzle hit)
  • Inscryption (card/strategy hybrid, viral appeal)
  • Into the Breach (small scope, high replay)
  • Wingspan Digital (friendly themes, eco-strategy)
  • Loop Hero (system-driven & addictive loops)
