What is Snarfles?

Snarfles is a single-player casual turn-based strategy game about balancing a simulated food chain. Currently in development, we aim to release on PC with campaign and challenge modes after a 6 month development period. Expect to:

  • Orchestrate predators, bunnies, and grass to keep an ecosystem alive
  • Offer sacrifices to Snarfles, a mysterious overlord
  • Strategize in a way that's deep yet feels accessible to anyone

Core Loop

Move

Position animals strategically to keep the ecosystem balanced

Eat/Reproduce

Build the food chain by eating other animals and reproducing

Sacrifice

Appease the Snarfles overlord by offering mandatory sacrifices

Survive

Keep your ecosystem alive for a set number of turns

Market & Scope

Target Audience

  • Casual strategy seekers: The same audience that made Polytopia a global hit
  • Fans of tight tactics: Players who loved Into the Breach's small grid strategy and unique interactions
  • Cozy campaign players: Story-first audiences who enjoy accessible, guided experiences like Outlanders

Unique Selling Points

  • Casual/accessible tactics game (rare in the strategy space)
  • Emotional Story Mode (15 levels, 2–3 hours)
  • Replayability: board-game inspired, procedurally generated challenge mode

Market Opportunity

The strategy genre is hot and underserved. Mewgenics sold 1M copies at $30 in its first week (2026). Other proof points:

  • Into the Breach: ~1M copies, $15
  • Polytopia: ~20M copies
  • Mid-range: Urtuk (~102k, $12); Hadean Tactics (~53k, $20)

Focused on sustainable outcomes as a small indie studio with a short dev timeline—not top-of-market.
